About Brown Bears

"The Brown Bear (Ursus Arctos) is native to parts of Northern Eurasia and North America. Its conservation status is currently<least concern. There are many species including the Atlas Bear and the Himalayen Brown Bear."

Features

Brown Bears are not always completly brown. Some can be reddish or yellowish. They have very large, curved claws and huge paws. Male brown bear are often 30% larger than female brown bears. They can range from 5 feet to 9 feet from head to toe."
